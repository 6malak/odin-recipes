Sandwich

Description

A classic sandwich consists of two slices of bread filled with various ingredients like meats, cheeses, vegetables, and condiments. One simple version is a ham and cheese sandwich, where layers of savory ham and melted cheese are nestled between toasted bread slices. It's a quick, satisfying meal perfect for lunch or a light snack.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham slices
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Mustard or mayonnaise
  • Lettuce or tomato

Steps

  1. Spread butter on the bread slices.
  2. Place ham and cheese between the bread slices.
  3. Add mustard or mayonnaise, if desired.
  4. Toast the sandwich in a pan until the bread is golden and the cheese is melted.
  5. Serve with optional lettuce or tomato.
