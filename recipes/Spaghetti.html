Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a popular Italian pasta made from long, thin noodles, typically served with various sauces. One classic preparation is Spaghetti Aglio e Olio, where the pasta is tossed in a light sauce of garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes. It's a simple, flavorful dish with a satisfying combination of textures and aromas

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (pasta)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic, thinly sliced
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper for seasoning
  • Parmesan cheese (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente, then drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© garlic until golden.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and stir briefly.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the garlic oil mixture.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and add parsley.
  6. Serve with optional Parmesan cheese.
