lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made of layers of flat pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It's baked until bubbly and golden, creating a delicious blend of flavors and textures in every bit

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (flat pasta sheets)
  • Ground beef or sausage (or a mix, for a meat sauce)
  • Marinara or tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese or béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Eggs (to mix with ricotta for creaminess)
  • Onion and garlic (for flavor)
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs like basil, oregano, and parsley
  • Salt and pepper for seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles and set aside.
  2. Cook ground meat with onions and garlic, then add tomato sauce and seasonings.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese with eggs and seasonings.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and Parmesan.
  6. Bake until bubbly and golden.
